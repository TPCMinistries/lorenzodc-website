For-Profit Holding Company

The for-profit arm is a holding company with multiple revenue-generating entities: the AI Incubator taking equity in trained entrepreneurs, the $50M Impact Fund deploying capital, education ventures, AI companies, medical operations, and other strategic business units. All generate market-rate returns while maintaining mission focus.

  • Holding company structure with diversified portfolio
  • AI and education companies driving innovation
  • Impact Fund deploying $50M in capital
  • AI Incubator taking equity stakes
  • Medical and healthcare operations
  • Target 20%+ IRR across portfolio
  • All exits create perpetual funding for nonprofit

The 10% Perpetual Flow

This is the innovation: After investor returns are paid, 10% of profits from ALL holding company entities automatically flow back to the nonprofit. It's legally structured, mandatory, and perpetual. As the holding company grows through multiple revenue streams, so does the nonprofit's capacity—forever.

  • Built into all holding company entities at inception
  • Flows after market-rate LP returns are paid
  • Not dependent on board decisions or donor fatigue
  • Creates sustainable, exponentially growing impact
  • Multiple revenue streams strengthen the flow
  • Aligns investor returns with mission outcomes

AI Incubator

The Process

We don't just throw money at problems. We build capacity first. Entrepreneurs go through intensive or accelerated training programs, mentorship, and pilot programs. We take 3-10% equity stakes in all participants—whether they receive fund investment or not. Only the best graduate to receive capital deployment.

  • 3-10% equity in all participants

  • Post-investment support continues
